    • How to Make a Japanese Cheesecake

    Japanese Cheesecake is a little bit different than typical American cheesecake. The texture is more soft like jiggling jelly. If you are curious how it tasts like, you should try to make your own Japanese style cheesecake now.

    Source credit:Japanses Cooking101

    Ingredients

    • 1 Tbsp unsalted butter
    • 6 large eggs
    • 300g cream cheese
    • 200 g heavy whipping cream
    • 30 mg sugar
    • 80 mg All-purpose flour
    • 50 mg sugar for egg white

    Instruction

    1. Seperate the 6 eggs into egg yolks and egg whites
    2. Set a medium saucepan water and bring to simmer. Add 300 g cream cheese, 1 Tbsp unsalted butter, 200 g heavy whipping cream, and 30 g granulated sugar in a large bowl. Put the bowl over the saucepan.
    3. Mash the cream cheese and butter until they are melted. use whisk to blend everything together. Remove from the heat.
    4. Add one yolk at a time in the warm cream cheese mixture add 6 egg yolks, make sure each egg yolk blended well with the mixing before adding the next one.
    5. Using a fine mesh strainer, sift 80 mg flour into the batter.
    6. add egg whites into a large bowl and slowly add 30 mg sugar in . Whisking it until the egg white has the firm peak.
    7. 9.Using a whisk, mix in 1/3 of egg whites into the cream cheese mixture until incorporated. Then gently fold in another 1/3 into the mixture.Lastly, pour the mixture back into the mixer bowl (with 1/3 of egg whites still in it) and fold in very gently. Pour the mixture into the baking pan in all at once, avoiding air pockets while pouring. Tap the cake pan a few times on the kitchen counter to release any air pockets in the batter.
    8. 10.Place the cake pan into the baking sheet thatâ€™s already in the oven. Reduce the oven temperature from 350F (180C) to 320F (160C) and start baking for 60 minutes. Reduce the oven temperature to 300F (150C) and bake for another 10 minutes.
